Welcome!

Time is precious. We may sometimes have to go through long paragraphs or articles, which may not contain all the useful information. If you read all of that content, you may waste your time in reading the inessential parts of the content. Our website is mainly intended to summarise such long paragraphs or articles by extracting the useful information from those long texts, thereby reducing the time required to read those and still get all the useful information, thus saving your valuable time. Just copy and paste such long texts in the box provided below and relax, we will summarize it and give you the important content from it.

Note:

Our text summarizer works better when the text you enter have atleast 30 sentences. However, there are no restrictions. You may enter text with any number of sentences.
